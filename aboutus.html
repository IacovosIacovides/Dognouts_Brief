About Us

Down to the last quid? The obvious thing to do is to start making and selling delicious dougnuts. Well, that's what we did back in 2015 anyway!

John Simpson and Harriet Jones kicked things off in mum's kithcen with a tenner and a wok. Before they knew it, they had themselves a micro-bakery and their own coffee and dougnut shop in Manchester.

We' re not talking your usual dougnuts either, oh no. These dougnuts are handmade coming riugh and ready with all manner of mouth - watering toppoings. We' ve even got vewgan dougnuts, because we' ve got your backs, you vegan champs. With Stores in the city centers of Manchester, Stockport, Bolton and Bury, these are dougnuts made different.
